1. Introduction

Welcome to Feelings, a mobile application created by Team Hazelnut. Feelings is designed to help couples communicate better, reflect together, and intentionally grow their relationship through shared activities, journals, conversations, and emotional check-ins.

2. Information We Collect

a. Account Information
Name, email address, profile image (Cloudinary), gender, love language preferences, and authentication data (Google / Firebase).

b. Relationship and Activity Data
Chat messages (text), voice messages and audio recordings, shared journals, private journals, secret notes, memory images, bucket list items, mood entries, questions, relationship insights, milestones, date plans, calendar events, and other in-app interactions.

c. Location Data
Approximate location may be used only to calculate distance between connected partners. Location data is not continuously tracked, stored, or shared.

d. Device & Diagnostic Information
Device type, operating system version, crash logs, and performance diagnostics collected via Firebase Crashlytics to improve app stability.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to enable core app functionality (such as chat, journals, voice messaging, reminders, and alerts), personalize the user experience, maintain security, improve performance, and provide user support when requested.

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal data. Your information is shared only: with your connected partner, with trusted service providers (Firebase, Cloudinary, Google Drive), or when legally required.

5. End-to-End Encryption

Feelings uses end-to-end encryption (E2EE) to protect sensitive relationship data between connected partners.

The following data is encrypted so that only you and your partner can access it:

  • Chat messages (text)
  • Voice messages and audio recordings
  • Shared journals and private journals
  • Secret notes
  • Encrypted references to images stored via Google Drive

For images stored in Google Drive, only encrypted references are stored and shared within the app. The underlying media remains accessible only through user-authorized Google Drive access.

Encryption keys are generated and managed on user devices. We do not have access to encrypted content.

During feature upgrades, previously unencrypted content may be securely migrated to encrypted storage using a background (lazy) migration process.

6. Data Storage & Security

Encrypted data is stored using secure infrastructure provided by Firebase and Cloudinary. Media files such as images and audio recordings are stored only to enable delivery between connected partners. Google Drive access is granted solely with explicit user consent and limited to selected uploads.

7. Data Retention & Deletion

You may delete individual content or your entire account from within the app. Account deletion permanently removes associated data from our systems. Due to encryption, some data may not be recoverable once deleted.

You may also request account or data deletion by contacting reach.feelings@gmail.com.
