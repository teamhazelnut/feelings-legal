## 1. Introduction
Welcome to **Feelings**, a mobile application created by **Team Hazelnut**.  
Feelings is designed to help couples build better communication, understanding, and creativity in their relationship through shared activities, memories, and check-ins.

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.  
This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### a. Account Information
- Name  
- Email address  
- Profile image (stored securely via **Cloudinary**)  
- Gender  
- Love language  
- Authentication data (via Google or Firebase)

### b. Relationship and Activity Data
- Shared memories (images stored in **your Google Drive**, shared via public links with your partner)
- Messages between users
- Personal and shared journals
- Mood
- Bucket list items (shared)  
- Questions answered  
- Relationship check-ins and insights  
- Favorite date ideas  
- Dates completed  
- Events and milestones  

### c. Location Data
- Approximate or precise location used **only to calculate the distance between partners**.  
- Not stored permanently or shared with any third parties.

### d. Device Information
- Device type, OS version, and crash logs for debugging and performance improvements.

## 3. How We Use Your Information
We use your data to:

## 4. How We Share Your Information
We **do not sell** your data.  
We may share limited data only:
- With your **partner**, for shared app features
